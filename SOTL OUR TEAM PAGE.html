OUR TEAM

Learn about the members of our team and our blog columnists that keep the content fresh and the podcast running smoothly!



Dylan Azcarate

Host

Dylan Azcarate is a lifelong resident of the San Francisco Bay Area. While having an expertise in Bay Area sports, Dylan has followed the NFL, MLB, and NBA closely throughout his time as a sports fan, taking a holistic approach to his takes on professional sports. Attending powerhouse high school Marin Catholic, Dylan played both varsity baseball and football—two programs which produce loads of talent and winning seasons in a competitive state for athletics. Being a lifelong fan of franchises that know how to grow rosters from the ground up, and a member of national champion rosters, Dylan has a keen eye for what it takes to be successful in any competitive environment. His experience being a part of athletic prowess gives Dylan an edge when it comes to picking winners and analyzing games. After graduating from high school Dylan elected to attend the prestigious Bates College in Maine.

Chris Cimino

Host

Chris Cimino is a resident of Maine and is an avid Boston sports fan (aside from the Bruins because hockey is boring). His fandom extends to the University of Michigan for college sports. Chris is a contrarian on the podcast and his negativity in regards to teams he roots for is unmatched. He makes sure to never be overly positive about his teams like Bay Area fans. He does not, however, bandwagon on certain teams like others on the show. Chris’s first love was baseball and, he had experience playing basketball and baseball in high school. He has since moved on to attend college in Lewiston, Maine at Bates College.

Luke Linnehan

Host

Luke “Linny” Linnehan grew up in a suburb just south of Boston, MA and is an active Patriots, Bruins, and Red Sox fan. He is anti-Celtic due to the extremely delusional fan base who are often referred to as “green-teamers.” Currently, Luke watches the NBA from an unbiased perspective despite it being rigged. His favorite college football team is the Florida Gators because his youth baseball coach played D1 Baseball for the Gators. However, Luke actively roots for Boston College in all sports as he has a long family tradition at the school. Luke is known to be very passionate about his sports takes and loves to debate. He participated in both basketball and baseball at Boston College High School and now currently is enrolled as a freshman at Bates College.


Emily DeLossa

Marketing & Public Relations

Born and raised in Los Angeles, CA, Emily has been surprising others with her support for east coast teams, mainly teams in the Boston, MA area, for a long time. In her younger years, she struggled to find a sport that she loved. Trying almost everything before finally finding her passion for sailing. Currently, she is a U.S. Sailing judge and sails in NEISA, a collegiate sailing circuit in New England. And, despite not connecting with many of the sports she played as a player, she found a love for each of them as a spectator. With a history in marketing and eSports management, Emily uses her talents to help the team in their social media pursuits and content/brand related endeavors. Emily currently attends Bates College.
